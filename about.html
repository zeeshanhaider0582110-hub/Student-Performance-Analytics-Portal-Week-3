About EduTrack Portal

A professional analytics platform designed to help educators track student performance, identify at-risk learners early, and make data-informed academic decisions.

■

Real-Time Analytics

Monitor grades, attendance, and assignment completion rates across all departments.

■

Early Alerts

Automatically identify at-risk students based on GPA trends and attendance patterns.

■

Smart Reports

Generate PDF or CSV reports for students, cohorts, or entire departments in one click.

■

Secure & Compliant

Role-based access ensures student data is protected and visible only to authorised personnel.

Our Mission

EduTrack was built to close the gap between raw academic data and meaningful action. Every educator deserves clear, instant insight into how their students are progressing.

By surfacing the right data at the right time, EduTrack helps institutions intervene earlier and build a culture of evidence-based teaching.
